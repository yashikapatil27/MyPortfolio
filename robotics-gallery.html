Robotics & 3D Printing Internship Projects

The end-effector for the IRB6700 robot was designed using Rhino7 and fabricated with a 3D printer. It is used to enhance the robot’s precision in handling objects during R&D tasks.

The ABB GoFa robot is holding a pair of scissors with a customized gripper, designed to grip objects securely. This setup was used during a ribbon-cutting ceremony event.
